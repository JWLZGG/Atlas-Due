# Founder Fit

## Why I’m building Atlas Due
I’m interested in the infrastructure layer between digital assets and real financial adoption, especially where trust, diligence, and counterparties remain bottlenecks.

## Relevant background
- web3 and digital-asset experience
- research-driven and ecosystem-facing work
- experience evaluating platforms, markets, and counterparties
- strong interest in products that help institutions and sophisticated operators make better decisions in crypto

## Why this market
As stablecoins, tokenized assets, and treasury workflows grow, wallet-level review remains too manual and too fragmented. That creates a real gap in operational trust infrastructure.

## Why this product
Most tools stop at raw analytics. Atlas Due turns review into a structured artifact that can be explained, reused, and attested onchain.

## Why now
Onchain financial activity is becoming more operationally important. That increases the need for reusable trust records around treasury, settlement, and counterparties.
